About Hollow Bridge

Copyright, ownership, and contact information.

Hollow Bridge is a creator-focused live stream automation app built to help streamers connect live platform activity to real-time stream actions, overlays, sounds, TTS, OBS controls, song requests, and other creator tools.

Hollow Bridge currently focuses on Twitch and TikTok live streaming workflows, with support for additional live streaming platforms planned for the future.
